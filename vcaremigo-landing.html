What We Offer

Everything your family needs,
in one place.

Built from the ground up for seniors — large text, high contrast, voice-first, and always responsive to what matters most.

Real-Time Location Sharing

Continuous GPS tracking lets family members know exactly where their senior is — on a live map, at all times, with battery-optimized background monitoring.

SOS Emergency System

One tap sends an instant alert with the senior's live location to every linked family member. Escalation logic and location snapshots are logged automatically.

In-App Family Chat

Encrypted messaging with image and voice note support. All media is stored securely on Amazon S3. Read receipts keep everyone in sync.

Upload and organize prescriptions, medical reports, and ID documents in one secure, permission-based storage vault — accessible only by authorized family members.

Multilingual AI Assistant

A RAG-powered AI model understands Telugu, Hindi, and English. Seniors can ask questions via voice or text and get contextual, simple, senior-friendly answers.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 8, 2026  ·  Operated by Taksha Infotech Inc

Introduction

vCareMigo is an elderly and family support system developed by Taksha Infotech Inc. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of every user — seniors, family members, and administrators — who interacts with our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what rights you have over your information. By using vCareMigo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Who We Are

vCareMigo is operated by Taksha Infotech Inc, registered in the United States.

Contact: team@codedale.tech  |  +91 7075631155

Information We Collect

1. Account & Identity Information

When you register on vCareMigo, we collect your name, phone number, email address, profile photo, and role type (Senior / Family Member / Admin). Authentication is handled via Google or Apple sign-in with OTP verification.

2. Location Data

vCareMigo uses continuous background GPS tracking to share a senior's real-time location with authorized family members. This includes live coordinates, location history, and location snapshots triggered during SOS events. Location data is only visible to users within the same family group.

3. Communication Data

Messages, images, and voice notes exchanged through the in-app chat are encrypted end-to-end and stored securely on Amazon S3. Read statuses are tracked to improve communication reliability.

4. Health & Document Data

Users may upload medical reports, prescriptions, and identity documents to the Central Storage (Documents Hub). These files are stored on Amazon S3 with permission-based access controls. Only authorized family members or the senior themselves can access these documents.

5. Reminder & Calendar Data

We collect information about medications, appointments, tasks, and scheduled events that users add to the shared reminder and calendar system.

6. Device & Usage Data

We collect device identifiers, operating system information, app version, push notification tokens, and usage logs for error tracking, performance monitoring, and customer support purposes.

7. Payment & Subscription Data

Subscription and billing information is processed through Razorpay or Stripe. vCareMigo does not store raw card or banking credentials. Payment processors handle data under their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve the vCareMigo experience. Specifically:

  • To authenticate and manage your account securely
  • To enable real-time GPS location sharing between seniors and family members
  • To power the SOS emergency system, including instant alerts and location snapshots
  • To deliver push notifications for reminders, SOS events, chats, and check-ins
  • To store and retrieve health documents and media files
  • To provide multilingual AI assistance (Telugu, Hindi, English) via our RAG model
  • To manage subscriptions, renewals, and billing
  • To resolve support tickets through the Admin Panel
  • To monitor app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Location Data — Special Notice

Because vCareMigo involves continuous background location tracking, we want to be fully transparent about how this data is handled.

  • Location tracking is on by default for Senior accounts once enabled by the account holder or an authorized family member
  • Family members can only view location data of seniors who have explicitly granted access
  • Location data is not shared with any third party outside the family group
  • You may disable background location permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will affect core app functionality

SOS Emergency System

When an SOS is triggered, vCareMigo immediately shares the senior's current location, a location snapshot, and an alert notification with all linked family members. This data is logged for safety and support purposes. SOS event logs are retained for a reasonable period to assist with incident review.

Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is stored on Amazon S3 with server-side encryption
  • All in-app communications are end-to-end encrypted
  • Authentication uses token-based secure sessions with defined token lifecycles
  • Role-based access controls ensure that only authorized users can view sensitive data
  • We conduct regular security audits covering tokens, roles, and storage encryption

While we take every reasonable measure to protect your data, no system is completely immune to risk. We encourage users to keep their login credentials secure and to report any suspicious activity to team@codedale.tech.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal compliance or ongoing support obligations.

Health documents and SOS event logs may be retained for a longer period at the request of the account holder or their authorized family members.

Children's Privacy

vCareMigo is designed for elderly adults and their adult family members. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at team@codedale.tech.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking or data processing
  • Download a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at team@codedale.tech. We will respond within 15 business days.

Multilingual AI Assistant

vCareMigo's RAG-based AI assistant processes voice commands and text queries in Telugu, Hindi, and English to assist seniors with reminders, health logs, and emergency guidance. Queries processed by the AI are used only to generate contextual responses and improve accuracy. Voice data is not stored permanently unless explicitly saved by the user.
